Residue Free

Generally during farming Farmers use Insecticide and other metallic ingredient to protect crop from environmental hazard,
during harvesting they harvest the product and take it to market for sale. The particle of insecticide, pesticide and other metallic material is called as Residue.

These Residue are consumed by us and then after long period it causes illness and other complications to our body.

From market it comes to your retailer where the Fruits and Vegetables face Co2 and Carbon monoxide emmision from Two and Four wheeler smoke.

We at Technofresh thoroughly clean the Vegetables with Nano Wash to remove the residues and other small infestation from the Agro products.
